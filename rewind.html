About Rewind

Who Are We?

Rewind is a Montreal performance group, with both a dance and vocal unit. This group was created in December 2021, in hopes of forming a safe environment where our members can share their passion and interests to the public, as well as to meet new individuals similar as them. Our goal is not only to grow as performers but also as individuals, going out of our comfort zone to be the better version of ourselves.

- Elizabeth Huynh, Leader of Rewind
