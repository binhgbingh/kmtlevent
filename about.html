We are a Montreal organization that focuses on the asian culture. For instance, Korean events.


Live your life to the fullest without any regrets.

Our non-profit organization was established in October 2019 with the name Meraki. Later on, it became Kmtl Event in late 2021 due to myself (Elizabeth) wanting to create even more events in the upcoming years. 

Our organization has created multiple events, such as Kpop random dance plays, movie nights, dance workshops, showcases (dance, vocal and instrumental performances), or even done collaborations for kpop nights or with other school clubs around the world, etc. Many more to come in the upcoming months and years. 

"To me, it is important to form a safe community where individuals from different backgrounds came come together to enjoy the same interests (dancing/music) as them, without feeling like an outsider."

- Elizabeth Huynh, CEO, Manager and Leader of KMTL Event
